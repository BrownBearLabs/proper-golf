Build

Overview

Building golf courses is my passion. I love being on site, on the machines, andgetting the details right. I take a lot of pride in the art of shaping and creating fun, interesting golf in the dirt. This highly involved process allows me to best execute the exact vision for the project, and allows the client to participate more actively in the architecture as well. Proper Golf is 100% hands on during the shaping process from start to finish. The process of building high quality golf requires nothing less.

Shaping is a blend of art work, and the ability solve the problems of making the design function strategically, stand up to mother nature and regular maintenance. Whether it is adding a small wrinkly contour with a sand pro just before seeding, bunkering into an existing scrubby sand dune, or delicatelyrestoring lost golden age features with an excavator, every step in this creative process is taken to ensure the long term success of the project.
