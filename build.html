Build

Overview

Building golf courses is my passion. I love being on site, on the machines, and doing the finishing details by hand. I take pride in the art of shaping and creating fun, interesting golf in the dirt. This highly involved process allows clients the best way to understand our vision for the project, and allows them to participate more actively as well. Proper Golf is 100% hands on during the shaping process from start to finish, as the process of building high quality golf requires nothing less.

I have been fortunate to learn from and work along side some of the best, most creative shapers in business. My shaping process comes from watching, and helping them solve the problem of making the design function strategically,

stand up to mother nature and regular maintenance, while maximizing the beauty. Whether it is adding a small wrinkly contour with a sand pro just before seeding, bunkering into an existing scrubby sand dune, or creating larger contours from tee through the green, every step in this creative process is taken to ensure the long term success of the project.

In that spirit, Proper Golf, can help assemble the ideal team for each project to contribute to defining its unique character. Proper Golf is truly a collaborative process. Jaeger Kovich is also available to partner with other architects who believe in the same principals of creating fun golf courses.
