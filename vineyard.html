Vineyard Golf Club - Martha's Vineyard, Massachusetts

Hanse Golf Design - Gil Hanse

Working for the first time with Hanse Golf Design and Caveman Construction, Jaeger Kovich spent 4 months rebuilding the back 9 at Vineyard Golf Club. What started as the remains of Donald Steel’s faux links, all the greens, bunkers and even the routing has been redone. The course now sits much nicer amidst the Martha’s Vineyard landscape.

Jaeger’s primary job was rebuilding all the bunkers, but after building trust with Gil and parter Jim Wagner, the role expanded. Jaeger is looking forward to more projects with them in the near future.
