Woodway Country Club - Darien, CT

Mark Mungeam - Mungeam Cornish Golf Design, ASGCA

As an intern for Mark Mungeam during college, Jaeger Kovich designed a 9 hole short course for Woodway Country Club in Darien, CT. Jaeger also worked on many different types of plans including: routings, individual hole drawings, drainage, and artistic renderings, as well as making site visits with Mark, and associate Tim Gerrish.
