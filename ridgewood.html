Ridgewood Country Club - Paramus, New Jersey

A.W. Tillinghast (Gil Hanse)

The opportunity to help Gil Hanse restore A.W. Tillinghast's Ridgewood Country Club was a perfect match for a Met Area native. Using photographs from 1935, when the club hosted The Ryder Cup, Tillinghast's bunkers, and green sizes have been restored. The famed 27 hole golf course will host the The Barclay's again in a few years, as well as the upcoming U.S. Girls' Junior for the USGA in 2016
