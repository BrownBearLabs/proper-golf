Heritage Creek Golf Club - Jamison, Pennsylvania

Kelly Blake Moran

A new 3 hole expansion to make a continuous 9 hole loop for local architect Kelly Blake Moran. The new 3 holes were routed through some environmentally protected parts of the property adjacent to the creek. Jaeger Kovich participated in this project while working for Pavelec Bros Golf Course Construction Company, Inc.
