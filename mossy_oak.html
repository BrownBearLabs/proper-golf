Mossy Oak Golf Club - West Point, Mississippi

Hanse Golf Design - Gil Hanse

Working again with Hanse Golf Design and Caveman Construction, Jaeger Kovich spent 3 months in the deep south shaping on the new course that is scheduled to open in 2017. Located on an old dairy farm, and under the motto of "Nature's Golf", many of the holes we built this spring sit gently upon the open prairie type landscape. With some high ridges and only a few trees exposing 360 degree panoramic views, there are going to be some really unique holes on the new course.
