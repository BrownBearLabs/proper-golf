Art

I have been photographing golf courses for years. It started out as a way to document some of the new courses I would play and study during college, as well as show friends some of the interesting courses I’ve played from around the world. From there it turned into a way to document my own work as I was starting out in golf course construction. Now it’s a full fledged artistic medium for me. I always carry the camera with me on courses either to help remember an interesting feature, reference a hole later on, edit some of my shaping work from a different perspective, or simply capture a beautiful image.

Over the years, my photo collection has become a rather extensive library for some of the best courses in the world. I have contributed pictures to the USGA Architectural Archives, magazines, websites, and clients for marketing material. I am always happy to contribute pictures from my library for the promotion of great golf courses. With the founding of Proper Golf, I, am happy provide my photography services on request to clubs, individuals, or events. (contact hyper-link)
