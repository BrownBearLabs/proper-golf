Art

Overview

As someone who participated in the fine arts throughout my schooling, it only seemed natural to combine my art work with my passion for golf courses. I have created this part of the webpage to showcase some of my better works from over the years. There are silkscreens from college, homemade yardage books, watercolors, and pages from my sketch books, along with a few samples from my large photo gallery.

I have been photographing golf courses for years. It started out as a way to document some of the new courses I would play and study during college, as well as show friends some of the interesting courses from my travels around the world. From there it turned into a way to document my own work as I was starting out in golf course

construction. Now itâ€™s a full fledged artistic medium for me. I always carry the camera with me on courses either to help remember an interesting feature, reference a hole later on, edit some of my shaping work from a different perspective, or simply capture a beautiful day on the course.

Over the years, my photo collection has become a rather extensive library for some of the best courses in the world. I have contributed pictures to the USGA Architectural Archives, magazines, websites, and clients for marketing material. I am always happy to contribute pictures from my library for the promotion of great golf courses. With the founding of Proper Golf, I, am happy provide my photography services on request to clubs, individuals, or events. Contact me.
