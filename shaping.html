Shaping

Over the past few decades architects have fallen into the habit of handing over millions of dollars in plans to be constructed by an outside party or contractor. Why pay all that money for the plans when it is only the very beginning of the process? We believe that the true design is only in its infancy on paper, and that it must evolve in the field. Every golf course and individual hole has countless problems waiting to be solved, and finding the best solutions can only be done by actively participating in the construction process. Proper Golf prides itself on the Design/Build concept, that Jaeger Kovich learned from mentor Tom Doak of Renaissance Golf Design.

Building golf courses is my passion. I love being on site, on the machines, and doing the finishing details by hand. I take pride in the art of shaping and creating fun, interesting golf in the dirt. Our highly involved process allows clients the best way to understand our vision for the project, and allows them to participate more actively as well. Proper Golf is 100% hands on during the shaping process from start to finish, as the process of building high quality golf requires nothing less.

In the spirit of Proper Golf, we will assemble the ideal team for each project to contribute to defining its unique character. Proper Golf is truly a collaborative process. Jaeger Kovich will partner with other architects who believe in the same principals of creating quality golf courses.
