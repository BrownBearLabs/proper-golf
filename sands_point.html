The Village Club of Sands Point - Sands Point, New York

Proper Golf - Jaeger Kovich

Now the consulting architect for The Village Club, Jaeger Kovich is working with the club on plans to reroute the golf course. The early Tom Doak golf course has been considering moving their clubhouse to be integrated with the Guggenheim Mansion, requiring a new start and finish to the course, as well as a new driving range. The Proper Golf plans call for a new opening hole to be played into the skyline of NYC, as well as a dramatic downhill par-3, and converting an existing hole into their new finishing hole. A community putting course has also been mentioned in their early stages of the project.

Jaeger will also be involved in the upcoming bunker renovation project the club is undertaking this coming summer.
