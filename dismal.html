Dismal River Club, Red Course - Mullen, Nebraska

Renaissance Golf Design, Tom Doak

Building The Red Course at Dismal River confirmed that the Design/Build approach is what I want to do for the rest of my career. With such a great site, and a minimalist philosophy there were just a few shaping opportunities for me, mostly on holes #8 and #13. When I wasn't shaping, I was transplanting native grasses, prepping for turf, or running our 5,000 gallon hydro-seeder. Dismal River's Red Course is the epitome of the team concept that Proper Golf brings to every project.
