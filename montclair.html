Montclair Golf Club - West Orange, New Jersey

Donald Ross, Charles Banks, Robert Trent Jones, Rees Jones

36 hole renovation led by architect and member Rees Jones. The project included rebuilding all the bunkers, 4 greens, as well as green expansions, tee resurfacing, tree removal and installing extensive new drainage. Jaeger Kovich participated in this project while working for Pavelec Bros Golf Course Construction Company, Inc.
