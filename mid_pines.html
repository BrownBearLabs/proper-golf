Mid Pines Golf Club - Southern Pines, North Carolina

Donald Ross (Kyle Franz)

The opportunity to help Kyle Franz restore Mid Pines back to the original 1921 Donald Ross bunkering, green sizes and sandy nature, was a great match for Jaeger Kovich, and the philosophy of Proper Golf. I spent most of my time restoring holes #7, 8, 12, 13 and 14, back to a state where Ross would be proud of his course just down the street from Pinehurst.

http://www.midpinesrestoration.com/

Golf Magazine 2013 Best U.S. Resort Renovation
