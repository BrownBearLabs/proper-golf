The Village Club of Sands Point - Sands Point, New York

Proper Golf - Jaeger Kovich

As consulting architect and shaper, Jaeger Kovich is rebuilding all the bunkers. The club had been seeking improved playability and conditioning in their bunkers for a long time. While remaining open for the season, and working with the in-house crew, Proper Golf has been adding some artistic touches that have long been missing from the early Tom Doak course.

With 15 holes completed through 2015, the club is still hopeful to make more substantial changes to these holes in the near future.
